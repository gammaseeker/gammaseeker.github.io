How does the system work?
Engineers will alternate between being the interviewer and interviewee.

Interviewers: Are to solve interview problems ahead of time alone with the most efficient solution. When the interviewer and interviewee meet, they will conduct a mock interview for the same problem. Other responsibilities include
  • Pick their head for what they know
  • Guide them if they get lost

Interviewee: Study data structures and algorithms. Interviewee is to read and understand problem day before meeting date. Responsibilities include
  • Ask questions to remove ambiguity
  • Discuss high level approach
  • Annunciate thought process as you code
